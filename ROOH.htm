ROOH-Ek Muheem:

It is an NGO of the college and though it is not a an individual society but in combination with Shiksha and Art of Living but all the three work independently. Its an NGO with the mission to realise the Rigth to Education, a good education for all. Under its initiative, it pays the school fees of the children of mess workers and other under priveledged children in and around NIT campus. Currently it has undertaken the responsibility of education of 30+ students.
It collects the funds from various modes like Newspaper collection, fundraisers, bookshelf and Sponsor a Child scheme for the proffesors. Apart from this, it also participates in other social works as well like Blood Donation Camps, Save Environment drives and energy conservation programmes. It also keeps track of the Swach Bharat Abhiyan in the college. It carries a Make A Wish event at Techspardha where it fulfills a wish of the children with the help of college students.

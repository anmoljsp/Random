Art of Living-

It is a campus extension of the Art of Living foundation of Shri Shri Ravi Shankar. It is not a full-fledged full time society but is only concerned with the conduction of a workshop called Yes+. It happens in both the semesters.
Even though it doesn't have a major existence in the college during most year, it is the part of the Social and Cultural society in conjunction with Rooh and Shiksha.
To know more about the organisation and their mission, you can check the website of Art of Living here @ Art of Living.

Techno(Technobyte):

The society of Computer department, it governs the technical coding events at techspardha. With the backing of a strong and hefty placement record, computer department is the most sought after with Computer and IT being the two most revered branches on campus. The events at techspardha related to computers are conducted by them usually in the Department of Computer Engineering. and the Center of computers and Networking.
The level and competitiveness of these events is top notch with some really testing and tempting coding events. It would be pretty tough for you to challenge the seniors in your first year but if you have good coding background, black box could be an event you could target. But participation is much more important as it makes you aware what you are upto.
It also hosts the game station in informals with popular games such as CS, FIFA16 and NFS. Last year, it also hosted the Google Glasses in the technical exhibition that takes place in the library.

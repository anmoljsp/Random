EMR(Embedded Systems and Robotics Club):

It is the Robotics and Embedded system society of the NIT-K. It is doing quite remarkably in the short tenure of its existence. Some of the robots have been quite exceptional with some unique achievements. Some of their significant projects include: gesture recoginition bot, maze solver and low-cost irrigation system.
Their workshop on robotics and embedded system is league apart from all the other workshops on robotics you will find. It includes a weekend of free lectures and a weekend of practical where you make your own robot,a line follower bot and learn to program the microprocessor. It happens around the Talent Show. Those who have attended the workshop are given preference while induction especially due to the more practical and technical knowledge than the rest of the applicants.

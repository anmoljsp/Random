JAM(Just Another Magazine)- )Its one of the couple of magazines of the college. Though the circulation is mostly limited to the friends of the publishers, but still they boast of some great content written exclusively by the people of the college and it also sometimes gives you very enchanting details of the college life.
Its circulation is an issue each semester and its cost is 150 Rs/-. So you might buy this forcefully due to your friend, but it would make for a good read. You get to know all the national and international events of importance in a nutshell in it. So yes it is not just another magazine. The inductions of the magazine takes place in the first couple of weeks.

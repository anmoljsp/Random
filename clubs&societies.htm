Clubs & Societies

Now you would have listened about clubs and the societies in the college circles… especially after the magazine inductions in the first couple of weeks you should have attended. So you must be wondering what the fuzz and buzz is all about. so I’m here to take you to a tour of the cultural and technical atmosphere of our college.
The foremost thing is what are the clubs and societies. Clubs are the groups whose domains fall in cultural and co-curricular spectrum while societies exercise their influence over technological and the social work of the campus. Their main objective is the conductance of the biggest and grandest festivals in the North India; except Delhi off course. The clubs undertake the responsibility of successful conduct of the Annual Cultural fest of our college “CONFLUENCE” and the annual cultural gala for the first yearites to showcase and hone their talents for Confluence called “The Talent Show”. The technical societies hold the Annual Technical fest Techspardha which is a national level program where top institutes of the country have compete against the best in the class. We have the technical fest exclusively for the first yearites called Altius, which was scrapped altogether last year. Due to clashes of duties during a fest, a person can join only one club and one society.

CLUBS

The main workforce and the wheels of any club are its first year. If the first yearites are passionate, determined and honest to their work, even sky isn’t the limit. It’s you who drive a fest forward. And a successful fest is important because it builds the repo of the college and off course gives you the bragging rights when it comes to your school friends… :D So, the inductions start just after the Talent Show. So participate heavily in the festival events of all the clubs as they only enhance you. Choose your club wisely but do choose one. It helps increase your interaction and opens new avenues to your personality. I hope I could be useful to you. Now let’s shift our focus to the technical societies.

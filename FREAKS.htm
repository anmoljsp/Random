Freaks- It’s a dramatics club, a non-official club but the official winners of the Nukkad Natak each year at the Confluence. And this has to be said that the judging is not always fair which is pretty sad as this is one event where all the participating teams are pretty great and very close to one another. So it can’t be said that they are not the rightful winners but they are not certainly the best each time.
All things apart, they are brilliant at what they do and even individually some of the members are great performers and have won the mono-acting events as well.
To watch them in action, you can see their performance at Confluence 2016 @ Freak's performance Conflu2k16.

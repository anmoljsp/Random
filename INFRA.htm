INFRA(Infrastrusture):

It is the official society of the Civil Department of the college and is the least active and the most subdued society of the campus. They conduct workshops throughout the year with the Auto-CAD workshop being their main workshop. During Techspardha, they usually have paper and presentation events as well as business plans. They offer you a great opportunity to participate as these are good events to start building your technical skill set. Apart from them, they conduct a couple of fun events which you can participate in while having a blast.
For more information, visit there facebook page @ INFRA,NIT Kurukshetra.
